👉About Us

Welcome to Q&A for Academics, a tool created by an academic enthusiast who is passionate about making knowledge more accessible. I created this tool because it can be helpful to teachers, students, researchers, etc. With Q&A for Academics, you can ask a question or provide a topic and upload a PDF document. Our system uses the Gemini 1.5 Pro API to deliver precise answers based solely on the document's content. Additionally, if the document contains any figures, diagrams, images, or tables, our tool will extract these visuals. My goal is to make the process of finding and understanding academic information straightforward and comprehensive. I hope that my tool will enhance your academic journey by providing clear and informative responses to your queries. Thank you for using Q&A for Academics, and happy learning!
